What is BERI?

BERI is a lab-style framework that can be customised to your experiences. It empowers students to take ownership of their learning journey through structured yet flexible research and exploration pathways.

Unlike traditional classroom models, BERI provides a dynamic environment where students engage with real-world problems, collaborate with peers, and develop critical thinking skills. The framework adapts to individual learning styles while maintaining rigorous academic standards and evidence-based practices.

Whether you're investigating scientific phenomena, exploring historical contexts, or tackling complex social issues, BERI gives you the tools and structure to pursue meaningful inquiry while building essential research and analysis capabilities.

Bespoke

BERI is a flexible architecture designed to adapt to different schools, policies, and learning environments. Swap in your own documents and experiences. BERI learns from what you provide.

Citation-Based

Manage and track authoritative sources, ensuring consistent performance and streamlined operations everywhere. Every answer is grounded in your school's official policies and documentation.

Data Privacy

Adapt to diverse markets with built-in localization for clear communication and enhanced user experience. Your data stays private and secure, with no external sharing.

Student-led

Generate precise, visually compelling reports that illustrate your growth trajectories across all regions. Students drive their own learning and exploration within structured guidelines.
