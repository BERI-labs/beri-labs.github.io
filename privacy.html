Privacy

Beri is designed to run locally on your device. Data you provide is processed on-device rather than sent to external servers as part of normal operation.

Data retention. Data processed by Beri remains on the device. It is not stored on remote servers by us. You can remove local data at any time by clearing your device's cache.

No tracking or analytics by default. Beri does not collect usage data, analytics, or personal information from students or staff as part of its standard operation.
